# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

UnisOrder serves Korean cross-border sellers operating Shopee and Lazada stores across multiple countries. They are often small teams or solo operators who need to process orders, print Korean invoices, track SKU stock, prepare purchasing, and understand margins without switching between country and shop dashboards.

## Product Purpose

UnisOrder connects scattered marketplace operations into one working flow: orders, inventory, purchasing, invoices, reporting, and margin analysis. The website should help sellers quickly understand the operational pain, trust the product's practical expertise, and move toward trying or contacting the service.

## Brand Personality

Clear, practical, and energetic. The brand should feel like a sharp operations partner built by sellers who know the daily work, not like a generic SaaS template.

## Anti-references

Avoid dark, heavy, abstract enterprise SaaS styling; decorative landing-page fluff; vague automation claims; and over-polished visuals that hide the actual product. Product screenshots should carry the proof whenever possible.

## Design Principles

- Show the real workflow before explaining it.
- Keep copy concrete and seller-centered.
- Use bright, clean surfaces with UnisOrder green as a confident operational signal.
- Make dense operational information feel approachable and scannable.
- Preserve mobile readability from the first pass.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast for public content and admin workflows. Avoid interactions that require motion, color alone, or precise pointer use. Maintain readable Korean line breaks across mobile and desktop.
